Nepal Telecom provides zero balance package free to needy customers for COVID-19 lockdown

Posted By nirajpal on 04/21/2020 tech talk

Who will benefit?

The zero balance package will not be available for all but for only those people who are deprived of communication due to no or less balance. Ntc has identified those needy prepaid customers by some criteria as those who made at least one call in the first week of lockdown (Chaitra 11 to 18). In addition to the above, the customer also needs to have less than 33 paisa mobile balance on the mid-night of Baishak 8, 2077. If both the criteria are met, then Ntc has sent a message to those numbers. The customer also needs to reply within 2 days to the same SMS as stated there, to get the package for free.

What’s in the zero balance package?

The package available in the zero balance offer is valid for 7 days which includes 100 MB data, 50-minute voice call within Ntc number, 25 SMS (on-net within Ntc mobile). All the numbers that do not fall in the above criteria receive those packages. Whereas Ntc declares that they may change the modality of such packages and the criteria as per the circumstances and requirement.

To start this service people just have to type Y and send the SMS to 1444, do so you will get above package free.

If you are one of those who do not fall in that criteria but have less balance, you can get Ntc loan. To start the service people just have to type START and send the SMS to 1477. To stop the service they should type STOP and send SMS to 1477. The maximum credit amount and status can be checked by using code STATUS.

Additional balance and validity

NTC also provides the package for free to the zero balance but not one-way barred numbers, from the lockdown data till now. They also add some minimum balance to the numbers to prevent it from barring one-way and increases the validity period until April 30, 2020.

Ncell bundled pack as a credit?

Find a similar offer of Ntc called zero balance package. All prepaid Customers having a balance of less than Rs 5 can take the pack for their regular communication.

Summary of Ncell bundled pack for lockdown

  • Price: Rs 5 (to charge later)
  • Data: 250 MB
  • On-net Voice: 25 mins
  • On-net SMS: 25
  • Validity: 7 days

Those people could not access Ncell service in this one month of lockdown and have a main balance of less than Rs 5. Those customers can activate the pack for their number whereas the usage period is only from 9 PM to 9 AM for 7 days of the activation.To activate the bundled credit pack for the lockdown, Ncell prepaid customers

  • need to dial *9988*55# or *9988# and then you will find the pack inside.

Ncell will deduct the price of the pack from the next recharge.
